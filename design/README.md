### Buoyancy Calculation
```
Volume displaced = Weight of canoe + Weight of crew + Safety margin
Target: Displace 400-500 lbs of water
```

### Weight Budget
- Concrete shell: ~50-70 lbs
- Reinforcement (mesh/fiber): ~5-10 lbs
- Hardware (seats, etc.): ~5-10 lbs
- **Total target:** <100 lbs

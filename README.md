- **Length:** 20 feet (competition requirement)

## Mix Design Philosophy

**Target:** 65 PCF unit weight with >1000 PSI strength

**Strategy:**
- Maximize Poraver content (reduce density)
- Optimize water/cement ratio (maintain strength)
- Air entraining admixture (lighter + durable)
- Minimal coarse aggregate

**Reference Mixes:**
- UC Berkeley 2019: 62 PCF, 1200 PSI
- Colorado School of Mines 2020: 68 PCF, 1500 PSI
- NAU Target: 65 PCF, 1000+ PSI

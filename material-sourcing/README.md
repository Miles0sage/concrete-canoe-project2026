- Lightweight alternative
- Check structural properties

- Industrial lightweight aggregate
- May require specialty supplier

## ğŸ“ Notes & Lessons Learned

### Key Insights
1. **Poraver doesn't donate directly** - must go through retailers
2. **Local suppliers are more responsive** - face-to-face works better
3. **Small quantities are perfect** - makes donation more feasible
4. **Educational angle is powerful** - suppliers like supporting students

### Best Practices
- Call during business hours (avoid Monday mornings)
- Ask for manager/purchasing department
- Be prepared to explain concrete canoe concept
- Follow up within 1 week if no response
- Send thank you notes for donations

## Why This Strategy Works

### Local suppliers are better because:
1. **Face-to-face builds trust** - They can see you're real students
2. **Convenience for them** - No shipping, easy handoff
3. **Community connection** - NAU is part of Flagstaff
4. **Small quantities** - Perfect for donations vs. bulk orders
5. **Recognition** - Local businesses like supporting education

### You're not asking for much:
- 10-20 lbs of aggregate (vs. tons for commercial projects)
- Educational purpose (tax deductible for them)
- Easy logistics (you pick up)
- Good publicity (you'll acknowledge them)

## Success Tips

1. **Be enthusiastic!** Your energy is contagious
2. **Be professional** - You represent NAU
3. **Be specific** - "10-20 lbs for a concrete canoe" is clear
4. **Be flexible** - Alternative materials are fine!
5. **Be grateful** - Thank everyone, even for "no" answers
6. **Be persistent** - It might take 10 calls to get 1 yes

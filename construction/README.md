## ‚ö†Ô∏è Safety Protocols

### Concrete Safety
1. **Skin protection:** Wet concrete is caustic - always wear gloves
2. **Respiratory:** Wear dust mask when mixing cement
3. **Eye protection:** Safety glasses during grinding/sanding
4. **Ventilation:** Work in well-ventilated area

### Workshop Safety
1. Power tool safety - training required
2. First aid kit on site
3. Emergency contacts posted
4. Clean workspace to prevent trips/falls
